about

Me and my brother like to bet. It’s not usuaully been with money, normally just pushups, a dead leg, or pride. Nothing is outside the realm of betting about. In order for it to work, the other person has to hold up their end of the deal, and be honest. Let’s just say my brother doesn’t exactly fit that description. That’s why the latest “I bet you can’t” right before I did really stung. He owed me 5 bucks. But of course, he suddenly didn’t remember and denied ever saying it. “We didn’t shake on it!” -_-. That’s when I started thinking about what I could make that would eliminate all doubts. It got me thinking about making a website just for bets, but an entreprenuerial switch flipped on and I decided that was too niche of a market. So I made the decision to go along with online polls, and try to make a platform that has simple functionality. Along the way I’ve picked up some great ideas, and here we are today!

Midzy is the best platform on the web for finding out what lots of people think. We do this through our fully capable polling methods and user-friendly website.
